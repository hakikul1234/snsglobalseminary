Frequently Asked Questions

When is the application deadline?

The application deadline is June 30 of each year. However, we accept applications throughout the year based on availability.

What is the entrance exam format?

The entrance exam is a combination of written tests in English, Mathematics, and Reasoning. For Kindergarten, it's primarily an observation-based assessment.

Is financial aid available?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for outstanding students and need-based assistance for eligible families. Contact our admissions office for details.

Can we visit the campus before applying?

Absolutely! We encourage campus visits. Please call us to schedule a guided tour with our admissions team.

What is the school day timing?

School hours are 8:30 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday to Friday. Extended care is available until 5:00 PM.
