Fee Payment Policies

📅

Payment Schedule

Annual fees are divided into three installments payable in April, August, and December for student convenience.

💰

Payment Methods

We accept online transfers, cheques, cash, and credit/debit card payments for your convenience.

🎓

Scholarships

Merit-based and need-based scholarships are available for deserving students. Contact the admissions office for details.

📋

Late Fees

A late fee of 2% per month will be charged on overdue fees. We recommend timely payment to avoid additional charges.

🔄

Refund Policy

If a student withdraws within 7 days of admission, 80% of fees will be refunded. No refund after 30 days.

🌟

Sibling Discount

A 5% discount on annual fees is available for siblings enrolled in our school.
